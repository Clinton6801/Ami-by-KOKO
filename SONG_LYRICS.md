# Àmì by Kòkò — Song Lyrics

All songs are sung by Kòkò the parrot.
Tempo: upbeat, child-friendly, 80–100 BPM.
Each song is 20–30 seconds.

---

## PART 1 — LETTER SONGS (A–Z)

Pattern for every letter:
```
[Letter] is for [African word], [African word], [African word]
[Letter] is for [African word], now you know the sound
[Sound] — [Sound] — [Sound] says the letter [Letter]
Kòkò loves [African word] every single day!
```

## PART 3 — MY WORLD SONGS (5 categories)

---

### Body Parts Song

Mouth and hands and feet below — Kòkò knows them all!
Touch your head, touch your nose
Touch your mouth, touch your toes
Head, shoulders, eyes and nose — Kòkò knows them all!

---

### Animals Song

Goat and chicken, dog and cow, parrot says hello!
Dog says woof and cat says meow — Kòkò loves them so!
Goat goes baa, chicken clucks
Cow goes moo, parrot talks
Goat and chicken, dog and cow, parrot says hello!

---

### Fruits Song

Mango, orange, banana too — Kòkò loves them, how about you?
Sweet and juicy, fresh and bright — fruits are Kòkò's delight!
Mango yellow, orange round
Banana curved and golden found
Mango, orange, banana too — Kòkò loves them, how about you?

---

### Objects Song

Cup and book and bag and shoe — these are things we use!
Ball and spoon and so much more — Kòkò knows them all!
Cup for drinking, book for reading
Bag for carrying, shoe for walking
Cup and book and bag and shoe — these are things we use!

### Weather Song

Sun is shining, rain is falling, clouds are in the sky!

When the rain comes after storm
Sun is shining, rain is falling, clouds are in the sky!
